Fire Lord Sozin

Fire Lord Sozin is the ruler of the Fire Nation and the one responsible for starting the war. Knowing that the current Avatar was to be born as an Air Nomad (the Avatar cycle continues in the order Fire, Air, Water, and Earth), Fire Lord Sozin first attacked and destroyed the Air Nomad Temples and it's people. Aang, being the Avatar, has to put an end to the war by getting rid of the Fire Lord and those who seek to wreck havoc to bring peace and harmony back to the world.

A Little More Information

Although Fire Lord Sozin was the one responsible for starting the war, he was later successed by his son Fire Lord Azulon who was the ruler for the Fire Nation for the majority of the war until he was assassinated by his second son, Ozai. Technically, the person Aang would be fighting against would be Fire Lord Ozai, but what started everything was Fire Lord Sozin's declaration of war 100 years prior.
