About Us

  We here at WildSketch Design love to be creative. We originally started out as a small group of 3 that would regularly meet up and draw together. Over the years we have grown through our social media platforms and posting our work. We have met a ton of really great people and are now a group of about 30. Our audience has wanted us to begin selling our works of art so now we have created a website where we can do so. We also added a way to get in contact with us about custom business inquiries. Thank you all for the continued support and we wish you all the best!
